Faculty

Hong Yu, PhD (PI)

Prof. Hong Yu is a Professor at Miner School of Computer Science at University of Massachusetts Lowell, Adjunct Professor at University of Massachusetts Amherst and UMass Chan Medical School. She is also a research health scientist at Bedford Veterans Affairs Healthcare System. Prof. Yu is an elected fellow of the American College of Medical Informatics. She received her PhD from Columbia University.

Postdocs

Feiyun Ouyang, PhD (Lowell)

Dr. Ouyang is a postdoctoral research associate at UMass Lowell since 2023. She received her PhD from Central South University, China. Her research focuses on epidemiology and influential factors of mental health and cardiovascular diseases.
